Product History

Orgonic Species has been delivering high-quality organic products since its inception. We began with a passion for sustainable agriculture and now provide a wide range of eco-friendly products. From organic seeds to herbal teas and natural fertilizers, our journey is dedicated to promoting a greener planet.



Lichi sweet fruit Seed

Product History

Every seed we offer is a testament to the rich diversity of nature, carefully selected for its ability to thrive in varying climates while maintaining its organic integrity. From the lush orchards of tropical regions to temperate zones, our Fruit Seeds ensure that each plant grown is pure, free from harmful chemicals, and enriched with natural goodness.



Con Seed

Product History

At Con Seed, every seed undergoes meticulous selection to ensure it meets our rigorous standards of purity and quality. Our product line spans across fruit, vegetable, herb, and flower seeds, all chosen for their ability to thrive in diverse climates and conditions while supporting biodiversity. With an unwavering commitment to sustainability, we empower both novice and experienced gardeners to grow their own food and flowers in ways that replenish the soil and foster ecological balance.

Nature-basket

Product History

Our journey began with a love for fruits—particularly their ability to nourish, refresh, and bring joy to people’s lives. Over the years, we've worked with local farmers and producers to ensure that every piece of fruit that reaches our baskets is a testament to nature’s excellence. From the juiciest tropical mangoes to crisp apples and the sweetest berries, Nature-Basket Fruits stands for authentic, farm-fresh quality.
